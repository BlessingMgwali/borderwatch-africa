POST A SHIPMENT page:
This is the core page for cargo owners.
Show a clean shipment request form:

Step 1 — Cargo Details:
- Shipment title (text)
- Industry/cargo type (dropdown):
  Mining Equipment, Agricultural Produce,
  General Cargo, Refrigerated Goods,
  Hazardous Materials, Oversized Load,
  Bulk Liquid, Livestock, Other
- Weight (tons) — number input
- Dimensions (optional) — L x W x H
- Special requirements checkboxes:
  ☐ Refrigerated (reefer truck)
  ☐ Hazardous materials certified
  ☐ Oversized/abnormal load permit
  ☐ Armed escort required
  ☐ GPS tracking required
  ☐ Insurance required

Step 2 — Route Details:
- Pickup location (text + country dropdown)
- Delivery location (text + country dropdown)
- Pickup date (date picker)
- Delivery deadline (date picker)
- Flexibility: Exact date / Within 2 days / 
  Within a week / Flexible

Step 3 — Budget & Preferences:
- Budget range: 
  Under R5,000 / R5,000-R15,000 / 
  R15,000-R30,000 / R30,000-R60,000 / 
  R60,000+ / Open to quotes
- Preferred carrier type:
  Any / Verified carriers only / 
  Enterprise carriers only
- Additional notes (text area)

Step 4 — Review & Post:
Summary of all details
Platform fee note: "BorderWatch takes 10% 
on the confirmed shipment value"
"Post Shipment Request" orange button

After posting:
Success screen:
"Your shipment has been posted!
Reference: BW-SHP-0234
Transporters will be notified immediately.
You will receive quotes within 2-4 hours."
